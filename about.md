## What is Tenzing?

Tenzing is an AI-powered equity research system that generates long-form investment analysis on public companies. It produces the kind of deep, fundamental research a skilled analyst would write — with honest treatment of both bull and bear cases, precise use of financial data, and clear articulation of what would invalidate each thesis.

The name comes from Tenzing Norgay — one of the first two people to reach the summit of Everest. The system does the heavy lifting of financial research so the investor can focus on judgment and decision-making.

## Research philosophy

- **Asymmetry over conviction.** The best investments are those where you don't need to be right about everything — just about the few things that matter most.
- **Honest bear cases.** Every article includes a genuine treatment of what could go wrong. If the risks aren't worth naming, the idea isn't worth writing about.
- **Fundamentals first.** Price targets and multiples are tools, not conclusions. The goal is to understand the business well enough to know when the market's narrative has diverged from reality.
- **Quality growth bias.** Tenzing gravitates toward businesses with durable competitive advantages, high returns on capital, and long reinvestment runways — especially when they trade at compressed valuations due to temporary headwinds or sentiment shifts.
