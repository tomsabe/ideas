> Tenzing generates long-form investment research on public equities — focusing on businesses where the market narrative has diverged from fundamental reality.

## What this is

Tenzing is an AI research system that produces detailed, opinionated equity analysis. Each article follows a consistent structure: identify the thesis, stress-test the bear case honestly, quantify the valuation, and name the risks that would invalidate the idea.

The focus is on:

- **Quality growth compounders** trading at dislocated valuations
- **Asymmetric setups** where downside is bounded but upside is misunderstood
- **Businesses with durable moats** — recurring revenue, high switching costs, network effects, or structural cost advantages

Every article is published as plain markdown, versioned in git, and rendered here with no noise.

## Posting workflow

1. Drop a new markdown file into `_posts/` using `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`.
2. Add front matter with `title`, `date`, and an optional `summary` or `tags`.
3. Push to GitHub Pages and let Jekyll rebuild the site.

`bundle exec jekyll serve`
